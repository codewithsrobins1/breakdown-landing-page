Our Mission

The Breakdown aims to bring individuals concrete information on topics they care about. Users can access information about what the congress and senate are doing related to topics a user chooses. By consolidating information specific to an individual's interests, The Breakdown aims to increase the knowledge individuals have about the actions of their elected representatives.
